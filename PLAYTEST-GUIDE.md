# Pixel Coloring - Playtest Guide

Welcome! This guide will help you set up and run the Pixel Coloring app on iPads and iPhones for playtesting with kids.

---

## What is this?

A pixel art coloring game where kids pick colors and tap to fill in pixel art pictures. It runs directly in Safari — no app store download needed.

---

## Opening the App

1. Open **Safari** on the iPad or iPhone
2. Go to: **https://dogacanaydin.github.io/pixel-coloring/**
3. That's it — the app loads in the browser

---

## Adding to Home Screen (Recommended)

Adding to the home screen makes the app run in fullscreen (no browser bars) and feel like a real app.

1. Open the link above in **Safari**
2. Tap the **Share button** (the square with an arrow pointing up)
3. Scroll down and tap **"Add to Home Screen"**
4. Tap **"Add"** in the top right
5. The app icon will appear on the home screen — tap it to launch

**Important:** This only works in **Safari**. It will not work in Chrome or other browsers.

---

## How to Play

### Home Screen
- Swipe left and right to browse the coloring sheets
- Tap a sheet to start coloring

### Coloring Screen
- A **"Tap to start!"** screen appears first — tap anywhere to begin
- The **color panel** on the right shows the available colors
- The currently selected color has a larger circle
- Cells that match the selected color will have a **green outline**
- **Tap or drag** on the highlighted cells to fill them in
- Each tap fills a small area (not just one pixel), making it easier for small fingers
- If you tap the wrong color, the cell will **shake** — just pick the right color and try again
- When all cells of one color are done, the app **automatically switches** to the next color
- The counter in the bottom-left shows progress (e.g. "50 / 400")
- Tap the **arrow button** in the top-left to go back to the home screen

### Finishing a Sheet
- When all cells are filled, the grid lines disappear and the finished artwork is revealed with a celebration sound!

## Sound

The app has background music and sound effects:
- **Home screen** has gentle background music
- **Coloring screen** has different background music plus tap sounds
- Sound plays through the device speaker — make sure the device is **not on silent mode**
- If you don't hear sound, check that the **volume is turned up** and the **mute switch** (on the side of the device) is off

---

## Tips for Playtesting

- **Hold the device in landscape mode** (sideways) — the app is designed for this orientation
- If the app feels stuck or looks wrong, close it and reopen from the home screen
- The app works **offline** once loaded — no internet needed after the first load
- Each device runs independently — kids can work on different sheets at the same time

---

## Troubleshooting

| Problem | Solution |
|---|---|
| App shows browser bars | Add to home screen using the steps above |
| No sound | Turn off silent mode (side switch), turn volume up |
| Screen looks zoomed in or cut off | Close and reopen the app |
| Sheets not loading | Make sure you have internet for the first load |
| App not responding | Double-tap the home button, swipe the app away, reopen |

---

## Device Requirements

- iPhone or iPad
- Safari browser
- iOS 15 or newer (most devices from 2017 onwards)
- Internet connection for first load only
